!start -- Creates a new game.  NOTE: Only one game can be played at a time.
!join -- Joins a game when a game is being created.
!play [number] [color] -- Plays a card.  Card numbers are done from left to right 1 through however many cards you have.  If you're playing a wild, you need to also type a color after the card number.
!draw -- Draws a card.
