**!start** -- Creates a new game.  NOTE: Only one game can be played at a time.
**!join** -- Joins a game when a game is being created.
**!play [color] [number/effect]** -- *!play red 6* -- Plays a card.
**!draw** -- Draws a card. (ONLY works on your turn)
**!uno** -- Calls Uno.  You have to do this before the next person plays, or you get hit with a 2 card penalty.
